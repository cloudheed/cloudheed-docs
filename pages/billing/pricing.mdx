## Billing Model

- **Billing Period**: Calendar month (1st to end of month)
- **Calculation**: Charged from database creation until deletion
- **Invoicing**: Automatic monthly invoices with line items per database
- **Currency**: EUR (Euro)

<Callout type="warning">
  Stopping a database does not pause billing. You are charged for the full duration from creation to deletion.
</Callout>

## FAQ

### How am I billed?

You're billed monthly based on usage. Compute is calculated hourly and summed at the end of each billing period.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes, you can change tiers anytime. The new pricing takes effect immediately.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards and SEPA direct debit for EU customers.

### Is there a free trial?

The Free tier is available permanently for development and testing. No credit card required.
